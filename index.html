Welcome!

My name's Tom and I'm an aspiring Full-Stack developer, currently making great looking websites as I continue my education.

For better or worse, technology is in my blood (I’m a millennial, sue me!), but for most of my life I’ve been a consumer, not a creator. I dabbled in Android bash scripts in my teens to expedite rooting (and re-rooting, and re-rooting, and re-rooting) my phones, but in the process of doing this never really put anything into the world I could stamp as my own creation.

Fast forward 10 years working in the bicycle industry and the itch to create has never quite gone away. It was this itch that rekindled a fire to go back to school to learn how the very structure of websites are created, and to start building my very own. This website is a love letter to the very technologies that allow this all to function, and I hope you like what you see!

Check out the different themes above and let me know which one you like best!

The bits I'm good at

Throughout my study I've collected skills in the above technologies, languages, and programmes. Whilst I can't yet call myself an expert I can say I'm well on my way!

Current Projects

Space Tourism

Frontend Mentor provided the assets and Figma layout files for this challenging space tourism site and I was then on my own to recreate it as accurately as possible. This was a delight to build and is the highlight of my development career so far. This site was built with CSS-3, HTML-5, Vanilla Javascript, and made use of some JSON for content population.

Todd The Fox Art

I was approached by Todd to build a landing page and general portfolio site to help him expand his influence digitally. The site is still under construction but makes heavy use of dynamic content changes on single pages to allow exploration into his artwork. Again, this site was built primarily with CSS-3, HTML-5, and Vanilla Javascript.
